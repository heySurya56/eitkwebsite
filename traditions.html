Traditions & Festivals

India is home to a multitude of festivals, each celebrated with immense joy and devotion. Festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Durga Puja are not only vibrant but also carry deep cultural and religious significance. These festivals highlight the country's rich traditions and unity in diversity.
