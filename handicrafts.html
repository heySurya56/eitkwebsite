Handicrafts of India

India is famous for its diverse range of handicrafts. These intricate and beautiful crafts vary across regions, from the delicate embroidery of Kashmir to the vibrant pottery of Rajasthan. Indian handicrafts showcase the artistic skills, creativity, and craftsmanship that have been passed down through generations.

Some of the popular handicrafts include Pashmina shawls, Madhubani paintings, wooden carvings, and metal works. These items not only reflect the rich heritage of the country but also make for unique souvenirs and gifts.
